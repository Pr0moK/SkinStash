<h1>SkinStash - App to monitor your CS2 investments</h1>
<h2>App still in build</h2>

What is app for?
<li>Tracking live investments in CS2</li>
<li>Get the lowest listed price at csfloat market and calculate profit</li>
<li>Shows actual lose/profit </li>
<li>In progress: function to sell items and track overall profit </li>
